## API-KEY

Visit the Google Cloud Console.
Sign in with your Google account.
In the top-left corner, click on the project dropdown.
Click "New Project".
Give your project a name (e.g., "Custom Search API Project").
Click "Create".
In the Cloud Console, go to the APIs & Services section.
Click "Library" from the left sidebar.
Search for Custom Search JSON API.
Click on it and then click the "Enable" button.
After enabling the API, go to APIs & Services > Credentials.
Click "Create Credentials" and select "API Key".
Your API key will be displayed. Copy it and store it securely in Line 67.
